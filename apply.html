Brightfield Tech Academy Student Application

Please use this form to sign-up for Brightfield Tech Academy. This will add you to the waiting list to enter the program. At this time we cannot provide estimates on when your class begins, but we will contact you at least 45 days before it is scheduled in order to begin the pre-work.

The information in this form will be used by KentuckianaWorks, the Workforce Development Board of the Kentuckiana region, to determine eligibility for the Code Louisville program and for anonymous reporting purposes on workforce and training programs in the Greater Louisville region.

IMPORTANT NOTE: If you have already applied before using this form, do NOT apply a second time. Doing so will overwrite your first application, moving you further back in line. Please contact apply@codelouisville.org if you have questions about your application.
