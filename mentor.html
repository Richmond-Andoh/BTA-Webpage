Interested in mentoring?

Mentors are an integral part of our success, enabling students to have access to those who have worked in the industry and want to share their knowledge and experience. While most of our mentors come from local companies, we also welcome remote mentors!

Brightfield Tech Academy is a program of KentuckianaWorks. It is funded by Oliver Mensah for Economic Development.
